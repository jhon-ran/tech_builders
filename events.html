Next meetup 📅 Mar 18

Product Hunt Launch Workshop

When: March 18 · 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Where: Nest Coworking · Playa del Carmen
Format: Practical workshop for founders and builders launching real products

This practical workshop is for founders, indie hackers, and startup builders who want to understand how Product Hunt really works. We will cover what the platform is, why it matters, how launches are prepared, and what helps products gain visibility, early users, and feedback.

If you are building a SaaS, AI tool, startup, or indie product, this session will give you a clearer picture of how to approach a Product Hunt launch without hype or guesswork.
