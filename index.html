Organizer

Built by a builder

I’m Jo Rangel, founder of Riviera Maya Tech Builders. I'm a polymath and indie hacker building products, experimenting with ideas, and learning as I go.

I started this community after meeting talented builders around here, but always randomly and briefly. The goal is simple: create a space where builders can actually connect, share what they’re working on, and maybe build something together. Riviera Maya has everything to be the next tech hub of Mexico, and the world. Help me make this happen!
