How we roll

Simple, friendly, and laidback

  • Monthly/bimonthly meetups (talks, workshops, informal sessions)
  • Conversation-driven — not sales-driven
  • Bring what you’re building (and what you’re stuck on)
  • Leave with feedback, ideas, and new connections
